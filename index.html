Cat University M.S. in Cat Studies

Overview Courses Faculty Admissions

Our Master of Science in Cat Studies (MSCS) program prepares students for high-earning careers in cat-related fields. Students will study in synchronous and asynchronous sessions and participate in hands-on experiential rotations throughout the program.

Cats 101

In this course you will learn how to feed, pet, and shelter a cat.

Cuddling and Snuggling

This course you will teach you how to properly cuddle with various cats according to their needs.

Advanced Chonkology

This course will teach you advanced caring techniques for larger cats.

Cats and Dogs

This course covers interspecies relationships between cats and dogs and methods for bringing the two together.
